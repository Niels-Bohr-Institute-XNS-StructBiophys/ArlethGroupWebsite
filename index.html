Welcome to the website of the Arleth Group at UCPH!

The group is situated at the X-ray and Neutron Science section at the Niels Bohr Institute, University of Copenhagen. It is headed by professor Lise Arleth, and specialises in the study of biological systems using small-angle scattering techniques, i.e SAXS and SANS. Almost all experiments are performed at large-scale fascilities, such as synchrotrons and reactors.
